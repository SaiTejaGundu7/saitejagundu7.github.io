Intro

Hi, I’m Sai Teja Gundu, a Senior Data Analyst with 4+ years of experience turning complex data into actionable insights.

I specialize in SQL, Python, R, Tableau, Power BI, and Looker, with hands-on expertise in building ETL pipelines, predictive models, dashboards, and real-time analytics using Spark and Kafka.

Across healthcare, logistics, and finance, I’ve designed solutions that improved forecasting accuracy, optimized operations, and supported executive decision-making. I’m passionate about data storytelling, automation, and AI-driven analytics.

Work

Over the past 4+ years, I’ve delivered impactful solutions across healthcare, logistics, and finance by combining analytics, engineering, and business strategy.

Key Highlights:

  • Built predictive models (regression, classification, forecasting) to improve retention, demand planning, and customer lifetime value.
  • Designed executive dashboards in Tableau, Power BI, and Looker that reduced ad-hoc reporting and enabled data-driven decision-making.
  • Automated ETL pipelines using Python, Alteryx, Informatica, integrating data across Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery, and Azure Data Lake.
  • Developed real-time analytics pipelines with Spark and Kafka, enabling anomaly detection and proactive monitoring.
  • Partnered with cross-functional teams to translate data into actionable strategies, driving measurable business outcomes.

About

I’m Sai Teja Gundu, a Senior Data Analyst with a Master’s in Business Analytics & Artificial Intelligence from the University of Texas at Dallas. Over the years, I’ve built expertise in data analytics, visualization, predictive modeling, and data engineering.

What drives me is turning raw data into meaningful stories that help organizations make smarter decisions. From building ETL pipelines and predictive models to designing executive dashboards, I enjoy creating solutions that bridge technical depth with business impact.

Beyond tools and code, I’m passionate about continuous learning, collaboration, and applying AI/ML to real-world challenges. My career spans industries including healthcare, logistics, and finance, giving me a broad perspective on how data transforms strategy.

When I’m not analyzing datasets, you’ll find me exploring the latest in AI, automation, and data-driven product design — always looking for innovative ways to make analytics more impactful.
